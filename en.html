Welcome to the POKéMON universe, a world of powerful and savage monsters with destructive powers that some humans try to capture to master their power. A few of them receive the official title of POKéMON Trainer. These compete in fierce battles to become the most powerful Trainer : the POKéMON Champion.

Relive the original adventure with an enhanced difficulty level and a completely new scenario. Explore the iconic locations of the Kanto region and battle its 8 Gym Leaders and the dreaded Elite Four. Will you triumph this time?

  • Greatly enhanced difficulty and AI that far surpasses the original games.
  • An upgraded scenario, many events and revisited maps.
  • An exclusive field system that opens up new strategies.
  • Addition of side quests and a new leveling system.
  • Great re-playability thanks to many game settings and NEW GAME+ mode.
  • NUZLOCKE and MONOTYPE challenges are already included and ready to be used.
  • Multiple easter-eggs for die-hard fans.
